About me

Well known talented ethiopian artist, Roba Junior.

Roba Tadele Jr. better known as Roba Junior is an upcoming Ethio-Eritrean R&B/POP/AFROSWING Artist. Roba is the first born of the Legendary Ethiopian Musician Tadele Roba Sr.

Roba started his Music career alongside his Father by featuring on some of his Father’s tracks. After that He started doing covers of other Habesha Artists like Mehamude Ahmed, Gossaye Tesfaye, Teddy Afro and even The Weeknd.

Photosh’en is his Debut Single which is been written and produced by Nati Turner and Roba Junior himself. The Video Clip was released on Hope Entertainments on that same year. It was the first Ethiopian Video Clip with actual fully coordinated cut scences, Which helped him to gain a buzz around the Nation. And Now back with his second release “Beselam New Wey”. A special project where he worked with the late Elias Melka, Ahmed Teshome and Beserat Surafel. Released on Minew Shewa Entertainment on YouTube and his personal account on Spotify.

He also announced he will been releasing his first Album on 2022.
